Professional Bio

My name is Gwendolyn Rehrig, and I am a postdoctoral researcher working with Fernanda Ferreira in the Ferreira Lab at the University of California, Davis. I study language processing and vision-language interactions.

I completed my doctoral work in psycholinguistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick, advised by Karin Stromswold, and I hold a certificate in cognitive science from the Rutgers University Center for Cognitive Science (RUCCS). I am pursuing an interdisciplinary research-oriented academic career.
