About

Professional Bio

My name is Gwendolyn Rehrig (pronouns: she/her), and I am a postdoctoral researcher working with Fernanda Ferreira in the Ferreira Lab at the University of California, Davis. I study language processing and vision-language interactions.

I completed my doctoral work in psycholinguistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick, advised by Karin Stromswold, and I hold a certificate in cognitive science from the Rutgers University Center for Cognitive Science (RUCCS).

I started a podcast called Manuscripted to make publications more accessible. Using the podcast, I will gradually record and release audio-friendly versions of my published work, as well as host recordings of other scholars doing the same.

You can listen to Manuscripted on Apple Podcasts.
